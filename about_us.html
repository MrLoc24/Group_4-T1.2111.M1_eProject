Story About Us

With over 35 years in the industry, Auto World has grown to become one of the nation’s go-to locations for the best in new vehicles. Offering great value for money and some of Ho Chi Minh city’s cheapest cars, Auto World delivers customers the largest variety of new cars all in the one location.
Auto World team is committed to providing the very best service to our customers. This is evident in the amenities we provide to ensure you have a great experience when you visit one of our sites. In addition to a huge range of new vehicles, our complexes include:
  • On-site café serving coffee, snacks and gourmet meals.
  • Auto World Finance Office, for all your insurance and finance needs.
  • Ample customer parking.
Auto World leads the way in providing a relaxed and friendly buying environment, where customers can bring the whole family. We believe that every family member should be able to share in the exciting experience of choosing and buying a new car, so bring the kids and make lasting memories at Auto World.
